Manage Fixed Price Services

Allow users to manage thier fixed price booking on HomeAdvisor's website and consumer application.

  • DELIVERABLE
  • Competitive Analysis Study, Sketching, Wireframes & Protoype
  • ROLE
  • Competitive Research & UX
  • TOOLS & SOFTWARE
  • Axure, UCD Research Methodologies, Decibel Insights & Adobe Analytics
  • PROBLEM
  • Currently, users on HomeAdvisor do not have the ability to self-manage their fixed price bookings. It falls on customer service, for those users who call in, to manage customers bookings.
  • GOAL
  • Create a better user experience for the fixed price bookings by allowing users to self-manage and edit their bookings on HomeAdvisor in order to alleviate the current pain points.

What is?

The image to the left (app on left, and mobile web on right), was our MVP approach of a fixed price booking detail view. The initial first phase was limited in functionality, users would have to call our customer service representatives in order to cancel, reschedule or modify the bookings details. For the time being, this caused many pain points which was not the ideal experience we wanted to present to our user base.

RESEARCH FINDINGS

The research phase began with a competitive analysis of other Gig Economy direct competitors. The Gig Economy sites that were analyzed (Handy, Takl, Thumbtack & Task Rabbit ) were all similar in theme and content, so it was essential to understand any differences in user experience when purchasing a cleaning service.

Analyzing, then synthesizing the data helped me understand the ideal approach that HomeAdvisor should work towards when allowing users to book and manage a project on either the app or desktop & mobile web. The analysis led me to conclude the following:

  • MOST IMPORTANT INFORMATION
  • Be clear and concise on what you want your users to understand and/or do
    Allow flexibility and self-management on all platforms
    Do not restrict certain functionality based on platform
    Provide honest and clear dialog
